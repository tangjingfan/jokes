Joke 1

Two friends got hit by a car. One of them lost his entire right side, the other lost his entire left side.
A bystander looked at the first guy and said, "Don't worry, you're all that's left."
Then the first guy turned to his friend and asked, "Are you okay?" The second guy replied, "Yeah... I'm all right."

Joke 2

A Polish airline pilot was flying toward New York. Over the intercom, he said, "Ladies and gentlemen, the Statue of Liberty is on the right side of the plane."
A few moments later, the plane crashed.
An engineer reading the report later sighed and said, "Well... all poles were in the right half-plane."
